## 🌌 What is COSMOCHAOS?

COSMOCHAOS is a fun, interactive age calculator that tells you:
- 🌀 How many **trips around the Sun** you've made
- 📅 How many **days** you've been alive
- 🔥 A **chaotic Gen-Z cosmic message** about your age
- 🌍 **Fun facts** from your birth year
- 📸 A downloadable **Galaxy Brief card** to share everywhere

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎵 Ambient space background music
- 🎨 5 cosmic themes (Default, Solar Flare, Nebula Green, Galaxy Pink, Deep Ocean)
- 🌌 Animated starfield + shooting stars
- ⚡ Warp speed page transitions
- 💬 200+ Gen-Z cosmic messages
- 🌍 Birth year facts (1924–2024)
- 📸 Downloadable Instagram-style Galaxy Brief card
- 👤 Personalized with your name
- 🌐 One-tap share to WhatsApp and more
- 📱 Fully mobile responsive

## 📸 How to Use

1. Click **START COSMIC SCAN**
2. Enter your name and date of birth
3. Hit **CALCULATE COSMIC AGE**
4. Read your cosmic message and birth year facts
5. Download your **Galaxy Brief card** and share it!
